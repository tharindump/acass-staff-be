Palm Leaf Manuscript Study and Research Library is a digital library project of the Faculty of Social Sciences, University of Kelaniya, Sri Lanka. The Library preserves palm leaf manuscripts found in Sri Lanka through digitization and provides access to them in order to facilitate study and research in the sphere of manuscript culture. It digitizes all types of palm leaf manuscripts irrespective of their subject coverage and thus serves as a home for traditional national knowledge.

The project is entirely funded by the Faculty of Social Sciences and funding from interested parties is accepted.
