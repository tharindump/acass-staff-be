Welcome to Construction Engineering management Division....

This division conducts a post-graduate course leading to PG-Diploma/MSc in Construction Project Management and provides the necessary Construction Engineering and Management input to the under-graduate and post-graduate courses conducted by the Departments of Civil Engineering and Management of Technology.

Apart from teaching and research, the staff members of this Division undertake consultancy assignments and provide advisory services to the industry. Construction Engineering and Management Division at Moratuwa is considered as one of the best places in the industry for management advice.
