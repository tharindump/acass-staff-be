Welcome to Examination Division

All internal Examination affairs of the University are coordinated by the Examination Division. providing rules and instructions on conducting written Examinations. In addition to Bachelor’s degrees, the Examination related to Diplomas and postgraduate courses are also conducted by this Division.

Convocations for all degrees, including affiliated institutes are planned and organized by the Examination in additional to the above Issuing degree Certificate and Transcript and sending Verification and individual result sheet and done by the Examination Division.
