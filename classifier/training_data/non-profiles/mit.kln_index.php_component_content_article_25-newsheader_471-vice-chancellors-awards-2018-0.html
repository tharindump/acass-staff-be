Vice Chancellor's Awards 2018

 

 

Dr. Thashika Rupasinghe, a senior lecturer of the Department of Industrial Management has won two prestigious Vice Chancellor's Awards at the awards ceremony on the January 2nd 2018. She won the award for the Outstanding researcher who published the highest number of research publications out of internal research grants within in the entire university. Furthermore, she won another award for the outstanding and selfless dedication and service in the successful execution of centre of excellence for strategic brand identity development activities. She was the recipient of the prestigious Vice Chancellor's Award for the Young researcher of the year in the faculty of Science last year.
