    The Faculty of Law of the University of Colombo is the only Faculty of Law in the entire University system in Sri Lanka under the University of Grant Commission. Situated in the heart of Colombo, based within the Island’s oldest university
  • Faculty of Law offers a four year programme of study, leading to the award of the Bachelor of Laws Degree
