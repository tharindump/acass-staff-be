The Colombo Job-Fair 2016

The Career Guidance Unit, University of Colombo a comprehensive career counseling and resource center dedicated to empowering students and alumni as active participants in their own career development. Our goal in the Career Guidance Unit, University of Colombo is to meet the career needs of university students.

Vision

Providing and training for University of Colombo undergraduates to embark on an optimal career and develop the necessary transferable skills including attitude, motivation and skills necessary to become efficient members of the workforce.

Mission

To help students identify strengths, motivations and purpose, and translate their ambitions into opportunities through professional connections.

The Colombo job Fair is open to all University of Colombo undergraduate students, graduate students and alumni, and is designed to help identify university of Colombo talent for full-time, part-time, internship, and volunteer opportunities. Traditionally, this is UOC’s largest fair drawing over 1,500 applicants andhundrads of employers each year.

Why you should visit us?

Career Guidance unit, University of Colombo is established to help undergraduate and graduated students choose and proceed on an optimal career path, based on the students ability, desire and available opportunities. Whether you have no clue what you want to do with your life or you already have your dream job planned out, your career guidance unit is a great resource for locating a job or internship.

Career Counseling

You can choose the career related topic you would like to discuss, whether it’s how to communicate your competencies, finding the right job search strategies or motivation. Career counseling is offered to University of Colombo Bachelor students who are looking for a full time job, part time job or internship after graduation. In order to get full benefit from the career guidance unit, we recommend that you attend to Job fair in 2017.

Register with the University's career portal

All students of University of Colombo can register with the Careers Services’ career portal MyCareer through which more than 200 companies and organisations advertise their jobs, internships, thesis projects and postdoctoral positions to Colombo University students. Click Here to register

For employers

We give employers the opportunity to find potential employees and work placement candidates from the large number students of University of Colombo. Employers can send email to advertise at our website (no cost). In addition to job vacancies, employers can advertise a subject suitable for internship or a thesis project, postdoctoral research position or traineeship.
