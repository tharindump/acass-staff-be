The Alumni Association of University of Colombo School of Computing
Welcome to the official web site of the Alumni Association of University of Colombo of School of Computing (UCSC). Alumni Association of University of Colombo School of Computing is formed in the sole objective of enriching the fellowship among Graduate and Undergraduate Community to initiate and hold responsibility to en route the development of UCSC in achieving highest educational, professional and ethical standards by acting as an independent entity.

The accomplishment of the association would also focus on the process enhancing the education standard and usage of ICT throughout the country. As a strong entity with having vastly talented professionals in the modern era, we the association, will make it our passion of achieving the above goals by accomplishing its predefined targets throughout.

Message from the President

Over the past two decades staff and students made several attempts to set up the Computing Alumni of the University of Colombo but had very little success. Twenty-fifth anniversary activities of the UCSC brought the Alumni together and an interim committee of the Alumni Association of UCSC was formed on 9th July 2010.
Through the alumni, our graduates could closely follow the Sri Lanka’s premier computing institute’s progress, activities and initiatives while keeping in touch with colleagues and advances in the field. Alumni could also contribute towards institutional development through participation, contributions, establishing new links with the industry and helping & guiding the students.
