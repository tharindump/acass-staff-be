WELCOME TO COMPUTER CENTRE

The Computer Centre of the Faculty of Medicine offers Information Technology courses for undergraduate degree programs, external degree programs, and post graduate degree programs. Moreover it conducts workshops and continuous professional development programs for Academic and Non-academic staff of the Faculty of Medicine and the staff of extended faculties. Computer Centre also provides Information Communication Technology services  for students and staff of the Faculty of Medicine.
