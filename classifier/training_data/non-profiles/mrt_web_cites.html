OUR VISION

CITeS will strive to provide and protect an environment that features, "IT Abundance," wherein IT infrastructure, services, and solutions are innovative, readily available, and utilized to provide exceptional support to students, faculty and staff in their endeavors to uphold the mission of University of Moratuwa.

OUR MISSION

In support of University of Moratuwa Strategic Plan, the CITeS mission is to provide, outstanding technology infrastructure, services and solutions that advance teaching and learning, enable research, empower staff to provide exceptional services, enrich the student experience, and effectively manage and protect institutional data.
