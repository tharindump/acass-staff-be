The University Statistics and Data Monitoring Unit was established based on a management committee decision of Quality Assurance Centre (QAC) of the University of Kelaniya. The Unit first started their works on 22nd of July, 2017 under the supervision and direction of Director QAC, the Dean of the Faculty of Medicine, Prof. N.R. De Silva. Present, the USDMU is functioned under the supervision of the Director, Dr. K.M.L.M.M. Gunarathne and the Assistant Statistician, Ms. U.A.G.W. Weerarathne.

The University Statistics and Data Monitoring Unit is responsible for conducting surveys inside the University and Collecting information to support for the various kind of reports requested from the University. The unit is mainly focusing on the QAC activities. Thus, necessary actions are being taken to support the QAC in taking decisions to enhance the quality of the University.

Continue your education and may you come out with remarkable achievements. University statistics and Data monitoring unit is always with you to give the fullest support if you have any statistical issues.
