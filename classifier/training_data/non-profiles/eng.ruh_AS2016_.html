The 14th Academic Sessions and 13th Vice Chancellor’s Awards Ceremony

Academic Sessions is an annual forum organized by University of Ruhuna to share research findings of staff and students.The 14th Academic Sessions and 13th Vice Chancellor's Awards Ceremony will be hosted by Faculty of Engineering, University of Ruhuna in 2017.

           Theme         :    Promoting Innovation through Research
           Event Date  :   1st March 2017
           Venue          :   Faculty of Engineering, Hapugala, Galle

The Organizing Committee invites Abstracts and Extended Abstracts for oral/ poster presentations for the technical sessions. The submissions are limited to Academic/ Academic Supportive/ Administrative/ Non-Academic staff and students of University of Ruhuna.

The Organizing Committee also invites papers to be selected for UoR Oration 2016.

Only online submissions are accepted.
