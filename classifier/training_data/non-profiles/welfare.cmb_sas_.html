Welcome to Sevaka Anyonyadhara Sangamaya Sevaka Anyonyadhara Sangamaya is a non-profit organization and the main welfare society of the University of Colombo situated on 20th September 1972.

At the begining we facilitate welfare of the staff members of the University of Colombo. But later due to requests of the members, we established our child society Co-operative Savings and Loans Society on 25th September 2002 to facilitate loan requirements and saving requirements of the members.
