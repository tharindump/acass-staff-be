APTITUDE TEST 2018

Bachelor of Science (Honours) Degree in Management and Information Technology (MIT)


The Department of Industrial Management, University of Kelaniya, Sri Lanka, the center of excellence in integrating management with information technology, offers you the opportunity of graduating with the four year B.Sc. (Honours) degree in Management and Information Technology (MIT), one of the most sought after degree programmes in the country.

Those who follow B.Sc. in Management and Information Technology degree programme have an opportunity for specialising in any one of the areas:

  • Information Technology
  • Information Systems
  • Business Systems Engineering
  • Operations & Supply Chain Management.

This degree programme is designed to fulfill the increasing demand for professionals skilled in information technology complemented with management expertise, where internship of 6 months duration and research/industry based project components are embedded in the curricula to provide the state-of-the-art undergraduate education to Sri Lankan students. If you are interested in being part of our vision and wish to join the programme you need to apply for the aptitude test. You are encouraged to apply online at www.mit.kln.ac.lk and send the documents stated below by post, indicating the reference number (generated when applying online) at the top right hand side of your attached documents. You may also apply by post using the prescribed format together with the required documents.

The application should include the following documents.

  1. A bank receipt for depositing Rs. 500/- paid to any branch of Peoples Bank to the credit of A/C No: 055-1001-30667553, Peoples  Bank, Kelaniya Branch.
  2. Certified copies of the results sheets for (a) G. C. E. (A/L) 2017 and  Z - Score (b) G. C. E. (O/L).
  3. If the names in any of the above documents differ from that of  application, an affidavit to confirm that they relate to one and the same person.

Applicants should possess minimum requirements, specified under section 2.2.8.2 of the handbook on "Admission to Undergraduate Programmes of the Universities in Sri Lanka for the Academic Year 2017/2018", issued by the University Grants Commission. In addition, those who apply to the UGC for admission to the universities are required to apply, sit and meet the minimum criteria set for the above aptitude test. An applicant is eligible to sit the said aptitude test if he/she possesses the following qualifications:

  • Obtained at least a “C” grade in one of the subjects; Higher Mathematics, Combined Mathematics, Mathematics or Physics

AND should have passed

  • three subjects at the G.C.E. (Advance Level) Examination in Biological Science stream or Physical Science stream

OR

  • two subjects either from Biological Science stream or Physical Science stream with Information and Communication Technology (ICT) as the third subject.

Applications by post should be on A4 size paper in strict accordance with the format given below and the perfected application should be sent under Registered Post, to reach the Deputy Registrar / Examinations, University of Kelaniya, Dalugama, Kelaniya on or before 28 February 2018. Mark "B.Sc. (Honours) Degree in MIT Programme" on the top left hand corner of the envelope.

Applications which do not meet the stipulated criteria and specified requirements will be rejected.

For inquiries regarding the application process / Aptitude Test : 011-2914482.
