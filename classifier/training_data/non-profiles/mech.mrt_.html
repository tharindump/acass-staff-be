Department Industry Consultative Board(DICB) Meeting - 2018

Department Industry Consultative Board (DICB) Meeting of the department of mechanical engineering for the year of 2018 was held at 22nd of February in the engineering faculty boardroom of the University of Moratuwa. All academic staff members of the mechanical engineering department and engineers representing various focus areas in the mechanical engineering industry were present at the occasion.

Completion of the Japan – Asia Youth Exchange Program in Science

A group of ten from the Department of Mechanical Engineering, University of Moratuwa was participated in the end of January for the Japan-Asia Youth Exchange Program in Science (Sakura Exchange Program in Science), which was implemented as a collaboration between University of Miyazaki and University of Moratuwa. This collaboration was initiated by Prof. Hiroki Tamura form Department of Environmental Robotics, Faculty of Engineering, University of Miyazaki and Dr. Y.W.R. Amarasinghe.
