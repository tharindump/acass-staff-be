DEAN'S MESSAGE

  

The Faculty of Medicine of the University of Kelaniya is set in a beautiful campus in the quiet town of Ragama, a few miles north of Colombo. We provide a multi-faceted environment that delivers high-quality education for those aspiring to join the medical and other health professions, as well for postgraduate students and trainees. Our staff members provide patient-oriented care through the university units at the Colombo North Teaching Hospital at Ragama, as well as a range of other diagnostic and therapeutic facilities that are directly run by the Faculty.Our academic staff members also engage in a range of basic, applied and clinical research activities, often with international collaboration and publication in high impact scientific journals.
