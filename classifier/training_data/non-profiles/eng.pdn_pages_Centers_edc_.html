Welcome to Engineering Design Centre , Faculty of Engineering

Engineering Design Centre (EDC) of the Faculty of Engineering, University of Peradeniya was established as a Science and Technology project of Asian Development Bank in the year 1993 and now it is entirely a self-supporting Centre in the university. It is a commercial arm of the University of Peradeniya and a part of the Faculty of Engineering.

EDC Professional Expert Services

  •   Independent body for investigation of Engineering matters
  •   Facilitator for industry to acquire new technology
  •   Project feasibility studies and management
  •   Design reviews in all Engineering disciplines
  •   Appraisal study of major structures – bridges, towers & buildings
  •   Field and laboratory investigation in Engineering and related disciplines
  •   Product development – design, manufacture and testing
  •   Productivity improvements
  •   Energy auditing & saving applications
  •   R&D work in all Engineering disciplines
  •   Continuous professional development programs
