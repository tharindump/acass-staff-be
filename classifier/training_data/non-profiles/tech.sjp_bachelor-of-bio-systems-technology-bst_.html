Introduction

An annual intake of 75 prospective undergraduates who followed GCE (A/L) Biosystems Technology stream will be enrolled for this degree program offer by the Department of Biosystems Technology. They are expected to be completing their Bachelor of Biosystems Technology degree in two focus study areas of Bio-Process Technology and Polymer Process Technology. The selection of these focus areas of study will based on the selection criteria;

  1. Preference to the focus study area
  2. Performances

Capacity to accept the undergraduates for the two focus areas are;

Polymer-Process Technology

Food-Process Technology 

Agriculture and Environment Technology 

Bachelor of Biosystems Technology (BBST) degree program is a fulltime four years professional degree program and the first year of study is dedicated to provide the undergraduates with necessary knowledge in basic sciences, pure and applied mathematics, and basic computing skills together with the Biosystems Technology oriented subjects.

Subject modules offered from the 2nd semester onwards of this program are aimed to provide Biosystems Technology oriented subject knowledge to the undergraduates. On the 7th semester undergraduates will be attending to industrial training / internship program as well as expected to commence their final year projects and to be complete all the course work modules, industrial training/ internship, and final year project by the end of the 8th semester.
