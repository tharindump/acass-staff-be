Postgraduate Research

The Department of Mechanical and Manufacturing Engineering provides facilities for postgraduate studies under the guidance and supervision of academic staff leading to the award of the Master of Philosophy (M. Phil). Further, the department is planning to start a PG Diploma/MSc program in Mechanical and Manufacturing Engineering on 2017.
