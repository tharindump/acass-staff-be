PROJECT IN A NUTSHELL

University of Kelaniya aims at achieving excellence through carrying out high quality research, strengthening the international relationship, cooperating with the community and the industry through providing intellectual capacity building in order to be in the forefront of higher education within the country as well as globally. It plans to facilitate the infrastructure development through “Era of Excellence Building Project”.

University of Kelaniya is stepping into the 21st century with this novel project. This is a multi-storey building project consisting of several modules which involve twelve concepts aimed at strengthening the institutional capacity of the University. This will create an asset which provides multiple benefits to the nation over the years.

Construction of the Multi Storey Building Project

University of Kelaniya is blessed with highly reputed historical background accompanied with the transformations took place with socio-economic dynamics. The University has its origin in the historic Vidyalankara Pirivena, founded in 1875 as a center of learning for Buddhist monks.

This project has been approved by the Cabinet of Ministers of the Sri Lanka Government as a Public Private Partnership project.Fifty percent of the total project cost will be provided by the Government while the balance has to be raised from the private sector and as donations from well-wishers including alumni.
