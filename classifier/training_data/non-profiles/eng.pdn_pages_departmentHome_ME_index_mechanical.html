The Department of Mechanical Engineering at University of Peradeniya, is one of the oldest and fully equipped departments in mechanical engineering in Sri Lanka.

Mechanical engineers need an in-depth understanding of scientific principles and engineering processes and they also need to be able to develop solutions to real-life problems.
