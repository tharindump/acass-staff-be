Inauguration Day 2017

December 20, 2017 in News

 

The Inauguration Ceremony of the newest batch arrival to the Faculty of Technology was held on 16th of December 2017.  All the staff members and seniors were there to welcome them to their university. This moment was also enlighten by the arrival of the honorable guests, Prof. Sampath Amaratunge vice chancellor of University of Sri Jayawardenepura , Prof. Kapila Perera vice chancellor of University of Moratuwa , award winning professor Prof. Dhammika Tantrigoda, Prof. Prashantha Mandawala dean of Faculty of Technology and alongside with all the other honorable guest
