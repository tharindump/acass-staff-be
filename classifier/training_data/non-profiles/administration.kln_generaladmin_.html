Welcome to the General Administration

The  General Administration Division is responsible for matters relating to the rehabilitation works, maintenance of buildings and equipment, providing services to the staff and students, procurement and communication facilities.

The following works are handling by this division.

Progress Review Committee meeting

This meeting will be held second and fourth Tuesday at 8.30 a.m. in the Senate Room to review the progress of the activities such as Procurement of goods, Rehabilitation and Maintenance, Landscaping, Establishments matters, Sports activities of students, Research activities,  University web site improvement, Career Guidance Unit's activities, Student Counseling activities, students' activities, Media Unit activities and other activities which are arising at that time.

Vice Chancellor, Deputy Vice Chancellor, All Deans, Registrar, Librarian, Bursar, All Administrative Officers and Chairman / Director / Coordinator of relevant Unit / Centre are the members of this Committee.

Assistant Registrar / General Administration will serve as Secretary of this Committee.

Deans' Committee meeting

This meeting will be held once a month i.e third Tuesday at 2.00 p.m. to discuss the administrative and academic activities of the University.

Vice Chancellor, Deputy Vice Chancellor, All Deans, Registrar, Librarian, Bursar, Works Engineer (on invitation) are the members of this Committee.

Assistant Registrar / General Administration will serve as Secretary of this Committee.

Annual Board of Survey on Fixed Assets and disposal of discarded items

Annual Board of Survey on Fixed assets will be coordinated and carried out by this division and further action will be taken to dispose the discarded items as per the UGC circular.

Rehabilitation of buildings

Rehabilitation of buildings are handling by this division as per the Procurement Guidelines and Manuals. Treasury allocated fund (101) and other funds such as Faculty Development Fund, University Development Fund, CDCE Fund, any other special fund, etc. will be used for this purpose.
