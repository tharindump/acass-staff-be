Administrative Activities
Vice Chancellor of University of Moratuwa with effect from Nov. 2011 to Nov. 2017.
First NDB Bank Endowed Professor in Entrepreneurship from Nov 2010 to Nov. 2011.
Elected Dean, Faculty of Engineering, March 2004 to March 2010 (elected twice uncontested).
Member, Council of University of Moratuwa from March 2004 to March 2010 and Chairman from December 2011.
Member, University Senate and Senate standing committees of By-laws and Higher Degrees.
Head, Department of Civil Engineering from May 2002 to March 2004.
First Head, Department of Management of Technology from Nov. 1998 to June 2002.
Team Leader of the Corporate Plan development Committee for the University of Moratuwa: 2001-2006, 2007-2012 and 2010-2015 (three times).
Board member, Institute of Technology University of Moratuwa from April 2004 to March 2010.
Board member, University Consultancy Services since inception until 2010.
