Academic Staff Profiles

Prof. K.K.C.K. Perera

B.Sc. Eng. (Hons), M.Sc., Ph.D.
Professor
Email: kapila@mech.mrt.ac.lk

 

 

 

 

Kapila Perera is a Professor in this Department who joined the University staff in 1995, also served as a Lieutenant Commander of the regular force of the Sri Lanka Navy prior to joining the Department. He obtained a Masters Degree from University of California Davis in 1989 in Mechanical Engineering, followed by Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering in 1993 from the same University. Prof. Perera's research interests include Turbulent Heat Transfer and Combustion Modeling, Energy Sources, Renewable Energy Technologies and Co-generation.

Teaching and Administrative Work: 

Teaching Areas:

  • Thermodynamics
  • Fluid dynamics
  • Heat Transfer
  • Energy

Research Interests: 

  • Heat Transfer
  • HVAC
  • Energy (Sources/Conversion/Conservation)
  • Combustion
  • Air Quality

Education and Training : 

  • BSc Engineering (Hons), University of Moratuwa, Sri Lanka - 1985
  • MSc Mechanical Engineering, University of California Davis - 1989
  • PhD Mechanical Engineering, University of California Davis - 1993
  • Fellow, Institute of Automotive Engineers, Sri Lanka
  • Member, Sri Lanka Energy Managers Association
  • Lieutenant Commander, Sri Lanka Navy (Retd.)
