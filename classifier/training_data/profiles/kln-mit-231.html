+ - Profile Click to collapse

Mr. A. S. Withanaarachchi is a Honors graduate in Industrial Management (2006) from University of Kelaniya and a Masters in Financial Analysis from La Trobe University (LTU), Melbourne, Australia (2010). He also obtained CIMA and SLIIT (Graduate Diploma) qualifications added to his list of academic excellence. Mr.Withanaarachchi has industrial experience as a consultant for IFS, a leading IT company in Sri Lanka over a three year period and head of the Information and Communication Technology (ICT) department of Toyota Lanka (PVT) LTD, a subsidiary of Toyota Tsusho Corporation, Japan for another two years. After serving as a Research Assistant at the Department of Industrial Management, University of Kelaniya, while perusing for MPhil in Management of Technology, Mr.Withanaarachchi joined the Department of Industrial Management as a Lecturer in year 2015.
