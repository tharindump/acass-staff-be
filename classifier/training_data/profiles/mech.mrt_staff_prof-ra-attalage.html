Research Activities : 

 

Ongoing Postgraduate Researches

  • Solar pond based thermal energy utilization for desalination of water
  • Aspects of building envelop for optimizing energy performance in buildings
  • Optimizing internal combustion generator based stand-alone hybrid energy systems
  • Analysis of building envelops to optimize energy efficiency as per code of practice for energy efficient buildings in Sri Lanka.
  • Heat exchange across multi-façade roof structures for residential buildings
  • Simulating performance of sub-systems of a combined cycle power plant
  • Analysis of CDM potential in thermal power generation sector of Sri Lanka
  • Modeling and verification of performance of an innovative Tea Dryer

Ongoing Undergraduate Researches

  • Intelligent lighting controlling system for office & residential environments
  • Development of a small scale vapour absorption system
