  Research Interest

  • Precision machining and accuracy measurements

  • Accuracy calibrations of Multi-axis machines and their accessories: Study calibration methodologies, Design and evaluation of rotary mechanisms (rotary axis), precision bearings, etc.

  • Physical sources of errors: study and identify the geometrical and kinematic errors, contact and elastic deformations, Thermal deformations and errors, and etc.

  • Modelling of machine tools and their accessories: application for design calculations of machines and machining processes, Simulations

  • FEM analysis : Ansys

  • Motion Controlling :Design cam mechanisms for controlling motions

  • Control and Automation

  • Manufacturing

  • Eco-designs
