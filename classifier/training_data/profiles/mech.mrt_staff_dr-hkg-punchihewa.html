Academic Staff Profiles

Dr. H.K.G. Punchihewa

 

10400  Moratuwa

Sri Lanka

himan@mech.mrt.ac.lk

Teaching and Administrative Work: 

 

Teaching: 

 

Undergraduate modules

ME4042- Production and operations management, ME 4072- Industrial engineering,  ME 4060- Industrial project management for BSc. Eng. final year students at University of Moratuwa; AYNYAF/3/CE/MR/AE/ME/02- Industrial engineering for 3rd year BSc. Defence studies students at General Sir John Kotalawala Defence University, Sri Lanka.

Postgraduate modules

ME 5102- Production and operations management, ME 5122- Quality management, ME5121- Industrial management and ME 5146- Human factors engineering (ergonomics) for students of MEng/ PGdip in manufacturing systems engineering at University of Moratuwa. Industrial energy conservation and management and Research methodology for students of MEng/ PGdip in energy technology at University of Moratuwa.

 

Research Interests: 

  • Industrial design and ergonomics
  • Driving and transport ergonomics
  • Industrial engineering
  • Engineering management
  • Product and process development methodologies
  • Lifecycle analysis
  • Engineering applications for marginalised/deprived communities

Research Activities : 

  • Development of a framework using principles of ergonomics and predetermined motion time systems to help people with physical disabilities to work in industry
  • Development of a methodology to design and manufacture suitable footwear for the primary schoolchildren in Sri Lanka
  • Explore the feasibility of cryogenic cooling in the Sri Lankan manufacturing industry
  • Development of an improved helmet for cricketers
  • Development of a selective tea harvester to suit the Sri Lankan environmental and geographical conditions
  • Design and construction of a bulk tea packaging mechanism
  • Design and construction of a cement block manufacturing to avoid health and environmental impacts

Education and Training : 

 

Education: 

PhD - Design and Ergonomics [Loughborough University, United Kingdom] 2010
M.Sc. - Industrial Engineering and Engineering Management [Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, Hong Kong] 2003
B.Sc. Engineering (Honours) - Mechanical Engineering [University of Moratuwa, Sri Lanka] 2001
Certificate - British Computer Society (BCS) 1998

Training:

  • Cleaner production: Five day resident programme conducted by the National Cleaner Production Centre-NCPC (2011)

  • Microsoft project: Conducted by Prof. AKW Jayawardane (University of Moratuwa) at University of Moratuwa (2005)

  • Introduction to Solid Works: Conducted by Dr G Karunaratne (Texas A&M University) at the Department of Mechanical Engineering, University of Moratuwa (2006)

  • Tender evaluation: Conducted by Eng. Jayantha de Mel (National Procurement Agency) at SLIDA (2005)

  • Advances in design- A systematic approach based on Axiomatic Design: Workshop conducted by Prof. Nam Pyo Suh (MIT) at Hong Kong University of Science and Technology, Hong Kong (2002)

  • COSHH trained for the control of substances hazardous to health [UK] - 2007, 2008

 

Career : 

 

Professional Affiliations: 

Student member: Institute of Ergonomics & Human Factors, UK [IEHF]
Life member- United Nations Association of Sri Lanka

Professional Activities:

  • Evaluator: Sri Lanka energy efficiency award organised by the Sri Lanka Sustainable Energy Authority. (2012 to date)

  • Health and safety consultant: Feasibility study on aviation fuel bridging project - Magampura harbour to Mattala airport. (May 2013 to December 2013)

  • Chairman of technical committee appointed by the ‘Ministry of State Resources and Enterprise Development’ to study on operational issues of the mill commissioned at Eppawala Phosphate Limited and provide recommendations for sustained functionality. (June / July 2013)

  • Member of programme development and evaluation committee for Sri Lanka Institute for Advanced Technological Education. (2012 to date)

  • Engineering study programme developer/Consultant: Newly established South Eastern University of Sri Lanka-Oluwil Campus. (2012/2013)

  • Chairman of organising committee for the ‘5th Japan-SAARC Energy symposium-JSES 2012’ held at Mount Lavinia Hotel, Colombo. (March 2012)

  • Development of instructional material for safe and efficient driving on highways for the Sri Lanka Police. (October and November 2011)
