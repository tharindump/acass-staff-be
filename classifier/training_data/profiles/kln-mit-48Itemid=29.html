Dr.Shantha Jayalal received his Ph.D in Computer Science from Keele University in United Kingdom in 2006. He holds a Postgraduate Diploma in Computer Science from University of Colombo and has a Bachelor Degree in Industrial Management from University of Kelaniya, Sri Lanka. Currently he is a Senior Lecturer in Department of Industrial Management of University of Kelaniya. Some of his research papers were published in prestigious journals such as International Journal of Web Engineering and Technology
